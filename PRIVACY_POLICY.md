# Deep Live Cam - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 19, 2026**

## Overview

Deep Live Cam ("the App") is a real-time face swap application for Android devices. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **NO personal data** is collected or transmitted to external servers
- **NO user accounts** - the app works completely offline
- **NO analytics tracking** to third-party services
- **Local analytics only**: Basic usage statistics stored locally on your device for debugging purposes

### Images and Videos
- All face swap processing happens **locally on your device**
- Source face images are stored **only in your device's app storage**
- Processed images/videos are saved **only to your device's local storage**
- **NO images or videos** are uploaded to any server or cloud service

### Permissions Required
The app requests the following permissions:
- **Camera**: To capture live video feed for real-time face swapping
- **Storage/Media**: To select source face images and save processed outputs
- These permissions are used **only** for the stated purposes

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:
- Source face images: `[Device]/Android/data/com.deeplivecam.android/files/`
- Processed outputs: `[Device]/DeepLiveCam/`
- Settings: Encrypted DataStore preferences (local only)
- Analytics logs: Local JSON file (never transmitted)

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT share any data with third parties.**

The app does NOT:
- Upload any images or videos to servers
- Send usage data to analytics services
- Share data with advertisers
- Transmit any personal information

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- **Delete source faces**: Clear from app settings
- **Delete processed files**: Use the in-app gallery or system file manager
- **Clear analytics**: Settings > Clear Analytics Data
- **Uninstall**: Removes all app data from your device

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following on-device libraries (no data transmission):
- **Google ML Kit**: Face detection (runs locally on device)
- **TensorFlow Lite**: Machine learning inference (local only)
- **CameraX**: Camera API (Android system library)

## Children's Privacy

The app is rated for ages 17+ due to deepfake content creation capabilities. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Responsible Use

### User Responsibilities
Users must:
- ✅ Obtain consent when using real people's faces
- ✅ Clearly label outputs as AI-generated/deepfakes

- ❌ NOT use for harassment, fraud, or malicious purposes
- ❌ NOT process inappropriate content

### Content Filtering
The app includes an optional NSFW filter (disabled by default). Users are responsible for appropriate use.
